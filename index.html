How to play:

  1. Naughts and Squares take turns to place their symbol on the game board.
  2. The first player gets three in a row, column or diagnal wins.
  3. Press New Game to restart a game without resetting results.
  4. Press Clear Results to clear all results and restart the game.
  5. You can pick the who starts the round before any symbol is placed on board.
  6. Click on AI play symbol for AI to takeover Square.
  7. Don't you just want to flip the table when you realised you're definitely going to lose?
